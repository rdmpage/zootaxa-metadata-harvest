A Checklist of the Moths of Alaska

Clifford D. Ferris, James J. Kruse, J. Donald Lafontaine, Kenelm W. Philip, B. Christian Schmidt, Derek S. Sikes

Pages: 1-25

Abstract

[en_US] This article represents the first published complete checklist of the moth taxa, resident and occasional, recorded to datefor Alaska. Seven-hundred and ten species are listed. General species distribution ranges within the state are included.Three North American records are listed for the first time: Cydia cornucopiae (Tengström); Eucosma hohenwartiana ([D. & S.]); Acronicta menyanthidis (Vieweg).

Alaska; checklist; Lepidoptera; moths; North America
