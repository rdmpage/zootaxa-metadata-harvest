A new Ankothrips species (Thysanoptera: Melanthripidae) from Iran with unusually short setae

Kambiz Minaei, Farinaz Haftbaradaran, Laurence Mound

Pages: 37-42

Abstract

[en_US] Ankothrips zayandicus sp. n. is described from Isfahan province, central Iran. It is unique among Ankothrips species, alsounusual among Melanthripidae, in having no long setae on the head and pronotum. Problems are discussed in defining genera in Melanthripidae and Thripidae on the basis of pronotal setae.

Keywords

Ankothrips; Isfahan province; Melanthripidae; new species
