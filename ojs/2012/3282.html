A new species of Artimpaza Thomson (Coleoptera, Cerambycidae, Cerambycinae) from China, with a key to the Chinese species of the genus

Lichao Tian, Li Chen, Zhu Li

Pages: 42-44

Abstract

[en_US] A new species, Artimpaza brevilineata Tian & Chen, sp. nov. (Coleoptera: Cerambycidae: Cerambycinae), is described from China, and a key to the Chinese species of Artimpaza Thomson is provided.

Keywords

the Chinese species of the genus Artimpaza
