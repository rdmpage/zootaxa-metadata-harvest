Abstract

[en_US] A taxonomic diagnosis of chironomid larvae collected from Precambrian Shield headwater streams, Canada, is providedfor 51 species. In addition, the Nearctic distribution is given for each species, including 17 new records, along with and information on their ecology and habitat.
