Review of Neotropical Chonocephalus Wandolleck (Diptera: Phoridae)

R. H. L. Disney

Pages: 1-54

Abstract

16 new species are described and a key to the males of 27 of the 28 Neotropical species known is provided (the omitted species being only known in the female sex). A partial key to the females is also provided but most are given code letters only until they can be linked to their males.

Keywords

Diptera; Phoridae; Chonocephalus; new species; keys; Neotropical Region

First record of Xyleborus atratus Eichhoff from Europe, with an illustrated key to the European Xyleborini (Coleoptera: Curculionidae: Scolytinae)

Massimo Faccoli

Pages: 55-62

Abstract

Xyleborus atratus Eichhoff, an ambrosia beetle of Asian origin, is reported for the first time from Europe, based on a series of females collected in northern Italy in April 2007 and 2008 in funnel traps baited with ethanol. A diagnosis of the species and a revised key to the genera and species of Xyleborini occurring in Europe is presented. An explanation is suggested for the large number of recent introductions of exotic ambrosia beetles into Europe.

Keywords

exotic pest; invasive species; ambrosia beetles; Asia
