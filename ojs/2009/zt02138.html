Checklist of the chewing lice (Insecta: Phthiraptera) from wild birds in Bulgaria

Mihaela Ilieva

Pages: 1-66

Abstract

[en_US] The checklist includes 282 chewing lice species belonging to 63 genera, 4 families and 2 suborders recorded in Bulgaria until 2008. Their hosts represent 194 bird species belonging to 48 families and 18 orders. Synonyms used in the Bulgarian literature are included.
